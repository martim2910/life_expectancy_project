Project: Life Expectancy Analysis

Purpose: Compare average life expectancy between developed and developing countries.

Data: Life Expectancy Data dataset from Kaggle.

Method: Group data by Status, calculate mean life expectancy, visualize with bar chart and include summary table.
Summary Table:
  Status      Average Life Expectancy
  Developed        79.2
  Developing       67.1

Results: Developed countries show higher average life expectancy (79.2 years) than developing countries (67.1 years).
